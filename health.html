Healthcare and how it is supported by technology

Scans
Although it now seems a simple fact, scans are very helpful in seeing what we cannot see: the internal intricasies of our biology and where it's gone wrong :/
Booking systems
Detailed patient information and timetabling are all stored, so that information can be shared between clinics and appointments made effieciently
Clinical testing
In silico drug trials are computer simulations that help with the development and evaluation of new medicines
Use of AI
AI and LLMs are now being used to inspect a large amount of medical images, for example, those of early stage cancer. This will allow diagnoses to be made sooner and therefore, save lives!
